Research: Geopolitics, focusing on U.S.-China relations and other related Asia-Pacific affairs;
Interpret Policy: To invite well-known scholars to interpret the policies of the U.S. and China, and release their findings to the public with the help of Chinese and foreign media platforms;
International Cooperation: To cooperate with the think tanks from the U.S. and Japan in international research projects;
International Visits:To organize for Chinese scholars, enterprises, and media organizations to visit foreign countries, and to organize visits for foreign scholars and associated personages to China;
Training: To offer training for people interested in critical issues in the international political, economic, and social fields.
