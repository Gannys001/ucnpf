John Gallagher: Rethinking the Strategy of Counterterrorism of the United States

 

1970-01-01 08:00:00  



8, Do you have any other concluding points on the growing threat of terrorism? 

 

The U.S. and China can work together to address the terrorism threat that is destabilizing key regions and the world.  In fact, terror groups want the U.S. and China to compete with each other, not to cooperate.  To build upon the “common ground” of counterterrorism / countering violent extremism will require compromise and a truly strategic approach on both sides, but doing so will allow the U.S. and China to apply our national power to reduce the problem significantly much sooner.  We must – as our citizens, and especially our children, deserve this.
