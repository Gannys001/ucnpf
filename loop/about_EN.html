About Us

The U.S.-China New Perspectives Foundation was founded in Los Angeles in 2016. In 2018 most of its functions were transferred to Washington, D.C., and it is now represented by offices on both the East and West Coast of the United States.

The goal of the Foundation’s founders was to improve and contribute to China-U.S. relations. The Foundation’s mission is to identify areas in which the two countries can work together, to identify and resolve differences between China and the U.S., to conduct policy research and hold conferences, and, mainly through educational activities and discussions, to achieve the goal of moving China-U.S. relations in the direction of friendly cooperation and increased mutual trust.

The Foundation is a 501(c)(3) non-for-profit educational organization approved by the Internal Revenue Service.
