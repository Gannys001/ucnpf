New Perspectives Magazine

Founded in 2004, New Perspectives is published by the U.S.-China New Perspectives Foundation. It replaced the earlier magazine Leader and features in-depth analysis of China-U.S. relations, Asian-Pacific affairs, and other significant global events. With its meticulous analysis and fresh perspective on these issues, New Perspectives provides a lens for examining Sino-U.S. relations as well as today’s major global challenges.

The magazine’s readership is mainly comprised of mainland Chinese scholars, high-level government officials (provincial department heads, directors of national government departments and higher, municipal government leaders), CEOs of large companies, heads of large private corporations, Directors-General of multinational corporations in the Greater China region, academicians from the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the Chinese Academy of Engineering, military command-level leaders and higher, high-level diplomats at foreign missions in China, as well as other individuals involved in Sino-U.S. relations and Pacific-Asian affairs.

New Perspectives is a magazine of the U.S.-China New Perspectives Foundation. The publisher, editor, and writers are top talent from various domains. The magazine is not available for sale to the general public and does not normally accept subscriptions.
